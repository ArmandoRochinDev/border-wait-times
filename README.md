# Border Wait Times App

A clean, intuitive app that lets users check real-time wait times at U.S.-Mexico and U.S.-Canada border crossings.

Border Wait Times is a free, open-source Android app that provides estimated wait times and open lane status 24/7 at ports of entry that process commercial vehicles, passenger vehicles, and pedestrians.

Wait times are broken down at each crossing by lane type, including Standard, Sentri, FAST, Ready Lane, Nexus, and more.
